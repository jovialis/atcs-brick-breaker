Brick Breaker.

A fun arcade game where you must hit the ball with the paddle toward the bricks. Each brick, once hit, will decrease in durability. Once all of the bricks have been destroyed, you may move on to the next level.

About the Project

This iteration of Brick Breaker was programmed for Advanced Topics in Computer Science at BB&N as part of the web development unit.
